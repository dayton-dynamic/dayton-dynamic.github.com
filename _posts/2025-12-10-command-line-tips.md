# December 2025: Command Line Tips, Tricks, and Tools

"$ ", says your screen.  Now what?

Have you ever seen another programmer tapping away at 
their command line, said "WAIT!  What's that you 
just did?", and learned by serendipity about a useful 
technique?  Let's try to make a bunch of those delightful 
discoveries happen in one meeting.  Think tips, tricks, 
tools, and so forth.

This is a stone-soup-style, bring-your-own-brain meeting; 
instead of a single presenter, we'll all share little 
morsels of knowledge.  Try to think in advance about what 
you have to bring to the table!  It can be for \*nix, 
Windows, or both.
