# October 2025: Mathalon: build it in Godot 

[Godot](https://godotengine.org/) 
is a free, open-source, multi-platform engine for building 
2D and 3D games 
with multiple programming languages.  It's used to produce 
[games like these](https://godotengine.org/showcase/). 

Special speaker Travis Small will show us how to rebuild 
a math game in Godot.  Follow along and build your own math 
flash card game!
