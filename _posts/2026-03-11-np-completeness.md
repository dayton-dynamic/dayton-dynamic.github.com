# March 2026: NP-completeness; special speaker! 

For March, we have a special speaker: 
Rodrigo Girão Serrão, travelling all the way to 
Dayton (through the magic of the internet, that is)

Rodrigo will take us through a beginner-friendly 
discussion of NP-completeness; one of those terms 
that computer science types use to terrify
the rest of us.  We can all benefit from getting 
past the jargon and learning how to think about 
these problems.

Rodrigo is creator of [mathspp.com](https://mathspp.com/).
He's an experienced speaker and tutorial instructor, 
author of several Python books, and producer of daily 
[Python drops](https://mathspp.com/drops) tips.
